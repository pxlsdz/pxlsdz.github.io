练习题

  1. What are two main purposes of developing operating system ?

  2. Are operating systems normally related to their manufacturers and hardware?

  3. List five functions of operating systems.

  4. What does buffering mean ?

  5. How multi-programs to be operated ?

  6. List three basic types of operating systems.

  7. What resources should be included in Resource management ?

ans：

  1. The primary goal is to make the computer system convenient to use. The secondary goal is to use the computer hardware in an efficient manner.

  2. Yes.

  3. (1) Job management; (2) Resource management;

    (3) Control of I/O operations; (4) Error recovery;

    (5) Memory management.

  4. Buffering means to overlap the input, output, and computation of a single job to improve system performance. It can increase the level of resources utilization and throughput capacity.

  5. With multiprogramming, several jobs are kept in memory at one time; the CPU is switched back and forth between them in order to increase CPU utilization and to decrease the total real time needed to execute a job.
